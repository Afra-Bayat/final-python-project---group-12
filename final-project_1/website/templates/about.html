Canadian Immigration Data

The immigration to Canada dataset available on Kaggle typically contains information about immigrants arriving in Canada over several years. The dataset provides details about immigrants coming to Canada from various countries over multiple years. It usually includes information such as the country of origin, continent, region, and the number of immigrants who arrived in Canada annually from 1980 to a recent year (usually 2013 or later, depending on the dataset).
